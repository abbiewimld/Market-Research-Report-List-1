<p><p>The laser flow cytometer market is characterized by two main types: bench top analyzer flow cytometers and high-speed cell sorter flow cytometers. Bench top analyzers are compact, user-friendly devices primarily designed for routine analysis of cell populations, providing essential data on cell characteristics. In contrast, high-speed cell sorters offer advanced capabilities to rapidly separate and collect specific cell types from a mixture, enabling more complex experiments and research applications. Both types are essential for advancements in cell biology and diagnostic processes.</p></p>
