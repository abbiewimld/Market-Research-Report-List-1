<p><ul><li>Blow & Blow</li><li>Press & Blow</li><li>Narrow Neck Press and Blow (NNPB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass forming machine market encompasses several key types: Blow & Blow, Press & Blow, and Narrow Neck Press and Blow (NNPB). The Blow & Blow method is primarily used for producing hollow glass containers, utilizing air pressure to shape the glass. Press & Blow combines pressing and blowing for more complex shapes, enhancing efficiency. NNPB specifically targets narrow-neck containers, using a precise approach to ensure quality and consistency, making it ideal for bottles and jars. Each method caters to distinct production needs.</p></p>
