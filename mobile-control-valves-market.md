<p><p>The mobile control valves market primarily comprises two types: monoblock valves and sectional valves. Monoblock valves are integrated units that combine multiple functions into a single block, offering compactness and ease of installation, making them ideal for applications with space constraints. Sectional valves, on the other hand, consist of individual sections that can be assembled as needed, providing flexibility in configuration and capacity. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing the control of hydraulic systems in mobile machinery.</p></p>
