<p><p>The Zinc Oxide Pigment market is primarily categorized into two types: powder and dust. Powdered zinc oxide pigments are finely milled and typically utilized in coatings, plastics, and rubber due to their excellent opacity and UV protection properties. Dust zinc oxide, on the other hand, refers to a smaller particle size that offers enhanced dispersion and is often used in cosmetics and personal care products. Both types contribute to durability, brightness, and stability in various applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
