<p><ul><li>Continuous Flue Gas Measuring</li><li>Non-continuous Flue Gas Measuring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flue gas measuring instruments market is divided into two main types: continuous flue gas measuring and non-continuous flue gas measuring. Continuous measuring systems provide real-time analysis of emissions, allowing for ongoing monitoring of pollutants like CO2, NOx, and SO2, which is crucial for regulatory compliance and operational efficiency. On the other hand, non-continuous measuring methods, often including manual sampling and analysis, are typically used for periodic assessments and are less resource-intensive, though they may offer less immediate data.</p></p>
