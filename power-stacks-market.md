IGBT stacks utilize insulated gate bipolar transistors for high-efficiency switching, commonly used in inverters and industrial drives. SCR and diode stacks employ silicon-controlled rectifiers and diodes for rectification and control in power applications, ideal for high-voltage uses. Other stacks may include various emerging technologies and hybrid solutions, catering to diverse industries like renewable energy and electric vehicles, enhancing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
