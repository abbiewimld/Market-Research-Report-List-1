<p><p>Mobile water treatment equipment for heavy industry consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Membrane Mobile Water Treatment** employs semi-permeable membranes to separate contaminants from water, ensuring high purity for industrial processes.</p><p>2. **Resin Mobile Water Treatment** utilizes ion-exchange resins to remove specific ions, effectively softening water and removing contaminants like heavy metals.</p><p>3. **Filtration Mobile Water Treatment** integrates various filtration techniques to physically remove suspended solids, bacteria, and other impurities, providing clean water for industrial applications. Each type caters to specific treatment needs and operational flexibility.</p></p>
