<p><p>Radiofrequency ablation generators are classified into monopolar and multipolar fashions based on their energy delivery methods. Monopolar generators utilize a single active electrode, creating a larger area of ablation with deep tissue penetration, making them suitable for various procedures. In contrast, multipolar generators involve multiple electrodes, enabling more precise targeting and reduced thermal spread, which is advantageous for delicate tissues. This differentiation caters to diverse clinical needs and enhances the effectiveness of various ablation therapies.</p></p>
