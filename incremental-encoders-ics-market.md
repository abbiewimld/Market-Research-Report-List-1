<p><ul><li>Reflective Encoders</li><li>Transmissive Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Incremental encoders are crucial in various applications for precise position and motion detection. The market can be categorized into reflective and transmissive encoders. Reflective encoders utilize a light source and a reflective surface to determine motion, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Transmissive encoders, on the other hand, require a light source and a photodetector on opposite sides of a disk, allowing for higher accuracy and resolution. Both types play vital roles in automation, robotics, and machinery control.</p></p>
