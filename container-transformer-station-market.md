<p><p>A Container Transformer Station (CTS) is a portable substation designed for power distribution, typically enclosed in a container for ease of transport and installation. The compact design and mobility of CTS allow for quick deployment, particularly in construction, mining, and emergency response situations.</p><p>The Container Transformer Station Market is witnessing significant growth, fueled by the increasing need for reliable electrical infrastructure and the rise of renewable energy projects.
