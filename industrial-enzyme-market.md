<p><p>The industrial enzyme market can be segmented into several key types: carbohydrases, proteases, lipases, and others. Carbohydrases are enzymes that break down carbohydrates, widely used in food and beverage industries for processes like brewing and baking. Proteases help in protein breakdown, essential for applications in detergents and meat processing. Lipases facilitate fat digestion, benefiting industries like dairy and biofuels. The "others" category includes enzymes like amylases and cellulose, catering to various sectors such as pharmaceuticals and textiles.</p></p>
