<p><p>Cyclone desanders are critical in separating solids from liquids in various industries. They come in two main market types: monoblock and multi-body parallel systems. Monoblock cyclones feature a single, compact unit, ideal for applications with limited space, providing efficient separation at lower costs. In contrast, multi-body parallel systems consist of multiple cyclone units working together, offering scalable solutions for larger operations with higher throughput demands. Both types optimize solid-liquid separation, catering to different operational requirements and capacities.</p></p>
