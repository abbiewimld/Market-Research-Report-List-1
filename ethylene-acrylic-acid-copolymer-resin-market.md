<p><p>Ethylene Acrylic Acid Copolymer Resin (EAA) is categorized into two primary market types: Extrusion Grade and Injection Grade. Extrusion Grade EAA is designed for processes involving continuous formation, such as sheet and film production, offering excellent flow and adhesion properties. In contrast, Injection Grade EAA is tailored for injection molding applications, providing enhanced mechanical strength and processability. Both grades are utilized in various industries, including packaging and automotive, due to their versatility and performance characteristics.</p></p>
