<p><p>The Neoprene Elastic Gaskets market is categorized into two main types: Saddle and Waveform gaskets. Saddle gaskets feature a rounded design that conforms to diverse surfaces, providing effective sealing in applications like plumbing and refrigeration. Waveform gaskets, on the other hand, employ a series of undulating patterns to enhance flexibility and compression, making them ideal for environments with varying pressures. Both types leverage the durable, weather-resistant properties of neoprene, ensuring reliable performance across various industrial applications.</p></p>
