<p><p>The steel seamless pipes market is primarily categorized into hot-rolled and cold-rolled steel seamless pipes. Hot-rolled seamless pipes are produced through heating and piercing steel billets, resulting in robust products suitable for high-pressure applications. Cold-rolled seamless pipes, on the other hand, are crafted at room temperature, providing enhanced precision and surface finish, making them ideal for intricate mechanical and structural applications. The choice between these types depends on factors like application requirements, strength, and finish quality needed.</p></p>
