<p><strong>The Photonic IR Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared</li><li>Long Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photonic IR Sensor market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR sensors, operating from 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, are ideal for biometric and surveillance applications. MWIR sensors, covering 3 to 5 micrometers, are used in thermography and gas detection. LWIR sensors, ranging from 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in thermal imaging and industrial monitoring, catering to diverse sectors like defense, automotive, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
