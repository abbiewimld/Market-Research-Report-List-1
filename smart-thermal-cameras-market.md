<p><strong>The Smart Thermal Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Thermal Cameras market is categorized into three types based on wavelength sensitivity: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave length cameras. Short-wave length cameras detect infrared radiation in the 0.9 to 3 micron range, suitable for industrial applications. Mid-wave length cameras operate between 3 to 5 microns, ideal for military and surveillance use. Long-wave length cameras, ranging from 8 to 14 microns, are primarily used for building inspections and environmental monitoring, offering greater sensitivity to thermal variations.</p></p>
