<p><p>The high-voltage bushings market encompasses three main types: composite bushings, compound-filled bushings, and condenser bushings. Composite bushings utilize advanced materials for enhanced mechanical strength and electrical insulation, making them lightweight and durable. Compound-filled bushings are filled with insulating liquids or gels to improve performance in harsh conditions. Condenser bushings incorporate capacitive elements, facilitating efficient voltage transmission while minimizing losses. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to the diverse needs of electrical transmission and distribution systems.</p></p>
