<p><strong>The Discrete Thyristors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Thyristor</li><li>Bidirectional Thyristor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The discrete thyristor market is segmented into unidirectional and bidirectional thyristors, each serving distinct applications. Unidirectional thyristors control current in one direction, making them ideal for applications like motor control and power switching. In contrast, bidirectional thyristors can handle current in both directions, making them suitable for AC applications such as light dimmers, heating controls, and phase control systems. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the application and the desired control over power.</p></p>
