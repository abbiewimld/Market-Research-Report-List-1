<p><p>The NEV (New Energy Vehicle) charging equipment market primarily comprises two types: DC charging piles and AC charging piles. DC charging piles, also known as fast chargers, deliver direct current to the vehicle, enabling rapid charging and reducing downtime significantly, making them ideal for commercial and high-traffic locations. Conversely, AC charging piles provide alternating current, typically offering slower charging speeds, which are suitable for residential and public charging scenarios where vehicles can be parked for extended periods.</p></p>
